EMERGENCY RESPONSE PROTOCOL

Level 1:
Minor anomaly detected.
Action:
- Monitor conditions.
- Increase observation frequency.

Level 2:
Gas concentration exceeds threshold OR pressure exceeds threshold.
Action:
- Notify supervisor.
- Pause non-essential operations.

Level 3:
Gas concentration exceeds threshold AND pressure exceeds threshold.
Action:
- Evacuate affected area.
- Activate emergency response team.
- Restrict equipment operation.

Level 4:
Critical risk condition during active maintenance.
Action:
- Immediate evacuation.
- Emergency shutdown procedures.
- Incident documentation and investigation.

Emergency teams must record all actions and maintain communication with the control center.
