INDUSTRIAL SAFETY STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE

1. Monitor gas concentration levels continuously.

2. If gas concentration exceeds safe thresholds:
- Stop ongoing maintenance activities.
- Restrict personnel access.
- Notify the control room immediately.

3. If pressure exceeds operating limits:
- Initiate equipment inspection.
- Verify valve integrity.
- Reduce operational load.

4. If high gas concentration and abnormal pressure occur simultaneously:
- Declare a high-risk condition.
- Evacuate affected zones.
- Activate emergency response procedures.

5. During maintenance operations:
- Ensure all permits are valid.
- Continuously monitor environmental conditions.
- Suspend work if hazardous conditions emerge.

6. Safety officers must review all critical alerts before restarting operations.
